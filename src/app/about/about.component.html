About Us


Bounce is breaking the cycle of sexually transmitted diseases through the use of technology.
Each year, there are an estimated 357 million new infections with 1 of 4 being STIs: chlamydia, gonorrhoea, syphilis, and trichomoniasis.
Bounce strives to be the basis of a brighter future, built on the shoulders of technology.
Bounce works to cut the chains created by STIs in Queensland and hopefully soon, all of Earth.
